General info

    WASD to move, E to interact, space to attack
    Move to stay online or load content if it isn't automatically appearing
    Items have stats according to their type, with a damage multiplier based on rarity (in order of most common to rarest it's
    grey green blue purple yellow).
    Damage and chance of hit increases with higher levels
    Attacks have a 1-(ac-attacker's level/40) chance of hitting and deal damage randomly, weighted by type and rarity
    Weapon types also have different ranges and attack speeds, displayed in your stats
    Different rarities of healing potions also heal different amounts
